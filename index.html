FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS/RINK PACKAGES

General Rink Questions

Q:   How do we buy skate tickets for General Admission?

A: We are open daily for 90 minute sessions. 8:30-10:00AM, 10:30AM-12:00PM, 12:30-2:00PM, 2:30-4:00PM, 4:30-6:00PM, 6:30-8:00PM, 8:30-10:00PM, and 10:30PM-12:00AM.

Q:   When is the Rink open?

A: We are open daily for 90 minute sessions. 8:30-10:00AM, 10:30AM-12:00PM, 12:30-2:00PM, 2:30-4:00PM, 4:30-6:00PM, 6:30-8:00PM, 8:30-10:00PM, and 10:30PM-12:00AM.

Q:   Do you have group rates?

A: We do have group rates if you can join us anytime between opening day and November 6th or from January 7th through the day we close the rink for the season. We do NOT offer group rates in our peak holiday season: Friday November 7th through January 6th. Rates for group skating are: Adults: $15, Children: $10, Skate Rental: $8. This requires a 15 person minimum. Please contact us at therink@patinagroup.com to make your booking.

Q:   Do you have Skating Lessons?

A: We do have group rates if you can join us anytime between opening day and November 6th or from January 7th through the day we close the rink for the season. We do NOT offer group rates in our peak holiday season: Friday November 7th through January 6th. Rates for group skating are: Adults: $15, Children: $10, Skate Rental: $8. This requires a 15 person minimum. Please contact us at therink@patinagroup.com to make your booking.

Q:   Do you have group rates?

A: No, there is no age restrictions for skating. Children under 6 must be accompanied by an adult.
