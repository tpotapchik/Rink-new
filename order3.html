PROPOSE AT THE RINK AT ROCKEFELLER CENTER

Ask your beloved to marry you at one of the world’s most iconic landmark locations, The Rink at Rockefeller Center. The sparkling holiday lights on the Christmas Tree and surrounding holiday spirit make for an unforgettable center-stage moment when popping the question. Get him or her to say “Yes!” with three dazzling VIP packages. Each offers exclusive ice time - when it’s just the two of you - and a celebratory romantic glide-for-two around a crowd of well-wishing onlookers. Our Silver, Gold and Platinum packages offer special details and touches, designed to make your proposal truly memorable and spectacular.

AVAILABLE PACKAGES
Silver | $350

  • Special skate passes for two(includes admission and skates) that let you bypass any waiting line

  • Playing your favorite song (you provide the CD) during your proposal and after when take a romantic glide around The Rink
  • A celebratory Champagne toast enjoyed rinkside
Gold | $500

Our Gold Proposal Package includes everything from the Silver package, plus

  • A bottle of Veuve Cliquot Champagne with two Riedel flutes, gift wrapped, and ready for you to toast when you choose – perhaps on your first anniversary?
Platinum | $1000

Our Platinum Proposal package offers you your first romantic meal for two as a newly engaged couple. Enjoy a Five-Course Tasting menu at either Rock Center Café or The Sea Grill, in addition to exclusive ice-time for proposing to the song of your choice and special skate passes to bypass any waiting lines. Glide off the ice, and let our skate concierge personally escort you to your table where you will celebrate with:

  • Guaranteed rinkside seating, directly overlooking the magical ice rink where the happiest “yes” of your life has just been uttered
  • A dozen roses waiting for you and draped across your table
  • A Five-Course Chef Tasting menu for Two
  • A bottle of Veuve Cliquot Champagne on ice upon arrival at rink side table
  • A leather-bound photo frame embossed with The Rink and holds two photos includes one pre-mounted Christmas photo of The Rink the other side is open for mounting an engagement-memory photo of your own

Q:   When is the Rink open?

A: We are open daily for 90 minute sessions. 8:30-10:00AM, 10:30AM-12:00PM, 12:30-2:00PM, 2:30-4:00PM, 4:30-6:00PM, 6:30-8:00PM, 8:30-10:00PM, and 10:30PM-12:00AM.

Q:   Do you have group rates?

A: We do have group rates if you can join us anytime between opening day and November 6th or from January 7th through the day we close the rink for the season. We do NOT offer group rates in our peak holiday season: Friday November 7th through January 6th. Rates for group skating are: Adults: $15, Children: $10, Skate Rental: $8. This requires a 15 person minimum. Please contact us at therink@patinagroup.com to make your booking.
